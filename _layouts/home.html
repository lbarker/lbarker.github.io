Lauren is a hybrid developer — a MarTech strategist, Full Stack Developer, and Front End Developer — with nearly 12 years of experience delivering solutions across all digital media channels. Currently, she leads web development and digital infrastructure strategy for Cal Poly Corporation.

She's launched Cal Poly Corporations first JAMstack website, manages HTML email communication and deployment for university-wide communications, and led the WordPress migration for 3 sites. She also leads a team of student developers, implements efficient development methodologies, and manages a team based GitHub workflow.

Prior to Cal Poly Corporation, Lauren worked at XYZ Textbooks as a front-end developer. She developed the XYZ eBook interface and executed user experience journeys from start to end.

While not coding, Lauren can be found surfing remote waves on the Central Coast or racing competitively with Pale Kai Outrigger.
